Ingredients:
Russet/Yukon Gold potatoes
Non-Dairt Milk (Oat milk works best)
Oil

Directions:
1. Boil water in a pot
2. Wash potatoes
3. Cut potatoes into chunks
4. Boil chunks until soft 
5. Strain water
6. Add non dairy milk + oil
(Any combination can work in my experience "whole" oat milk with butter flavored coconut oil works best)
7. Mash
Optional: Whisk if you want them extra fluffy
8. Serve
